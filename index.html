Our Signature Glass Products

Insulated Glass Units (IGU)

Energy-efficient double and triple pane glass for superior thermal performance and noise reduction.

Tempered Safety Glass

Four times stronger than standard glass. Essential for shower enclosures, railings, and commercial doors.

Custom Cut Mirrors

Precision-cut mirrors for bathrooms, gyms, and decorative wall installations. Polished edges available.

DGU Glass

Its energy-saving properties align with green building practices and contribute to achieving sustainability goals. Double Glazing Units (DGU) offer numerous benefits, including enhanced thermal insulation, noise reduction, condensation control, and UV protection.

Laminated Glass

Laminated glass is manufactured by bonding two or more layers of glass together with an interlayer, such as PVB, under heat and pressure, to create a single sheet of glass.
